Who we are

A Family That Lives to Travel

Founded in 2008 in Navi Mumbai, Avadhut Tours started with a single vision — to make quality travel accessible to every Indian family. What began as a small group tour operation has grown into a full-service travel company handling 500+ departures each year.

We specialise in customised itineraries — no fixed routes, no generic packages. You tell us your destination, dates, group size and budget; we build a trip that fits perfectly. From a couple's honeymoon in Kashmir to a 100-member corporate group to Ladakh — we handle it all.

Our team of experienced tour managers, well-mannered drivers and trusted hotel partners ensures that every traveller returns home with wonderful memories — and books their next trip with us.
