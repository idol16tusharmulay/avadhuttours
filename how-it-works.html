Customised vs Fixed-departure group tours

Why We Don't Run Group Departures

Big operators sell pre-packaged group tours with fixed dates. They're cheaper per head — but you travel on their schedule, with strangers, at their pace. Here's how the two compare:

Avadhut (Customised)Fixed Group Tours
Travel datesPick any dayFixed monthly departures
Group compositionJust your groupMixed with strangers
PaceSlower / faster as you wishFixed by operator

Both models work — fixed-group tours suit solo travellers and those on tight budgets. Customised suits families, couples, corporate groups and anyone who values control over cost.
